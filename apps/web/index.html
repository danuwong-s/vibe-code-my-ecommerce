The Story

Dr. Fahlada (Lingling Kwong), known as the "Angel Doctor," works at a prestigious hospital owned by her family. Before her medical career, she studied abroad where she fell deeply in love with Earn (Orm Kornnaphat).

The pair were together for three beautiful years before Earn abruptly ended the relationship without explanation, telling Fahlada she "doesn't like women" and "only likes men." Heartbroken, Fahlada tried to move on with her life.

Years later, their paths cross again when Earn becomes the brand spokesperson for Fahlada's hospital. Earn tells Fahlada she missed her, and their reunion becomes passionate as former flames reignite.

But Fahlada harbors a secret—she wants Earn to know "how it feels to be ditched." Will their love story find a happy ending, or will past wounds destroy their second chance?
