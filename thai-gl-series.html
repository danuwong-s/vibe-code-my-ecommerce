The Story

In the misty mountains of Chiang Mai, where ancient temples meet blooming flower farms, two young women are about to discover that love knows no boundaries.

Nari, a talented botanical illustrator, has always found solace in nature's beauty. Her world is peaceful until Kan, a fiercely ambitious fashion designer from Bangkok, arrives seeking inspiration for her new collection.

As Kan immerses herself in Chiang Mai's rich culture, she finds herself drawn not just to the scenery, but to the quiet artist who sees the world through petals and paint. Their connection blooms like the flowers Nari paints—beautiful, unexpected, and impossible to ignore.

But their journey isn't without challenges. Family expectations, societal norms, and their own fears threaten to keep them apart. Will they find the courage to love openly, or will their first love remain a beautiful painting—captured but never fully lived?
